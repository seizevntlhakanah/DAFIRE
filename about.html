About DAFIRE

Mission

Through innovative projects such as recycled frames made from cupboard
materials and expressive watercolor paintings, we aim to show that art
can be both beautiful and responsible.
DAFIRE encourages individuals to see potential in discarded
items and turn them into something extraordinary.

Our Story

DAFIRE is started in 2026 with the mission of bringing love for
aesthetics and decorating our houses and spaces with simple based art.

Our Vision

Our team consists of passionate Artists, Marketing Strategists,
Designers and Public Relations Practitioners working together to create
high-quality,unique artwork for our community.
